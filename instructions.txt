Classic Grilled Cheese Sandwich Recipe:
1. Gather your ingredients: bread, cheese, butter.
2. Butter one side of each bread slice.
3. Place a slice of cheese between the unbuttered sides of the bread slices.
4. Heat a pan over medium heat.
5. Once the pan is hot, place the sandwich in the pan, buttered side down.
6. Cook for about 3-4 minutes on each side or until golden brown and the cheese has melted.
7. Remove from pan, slice diagonally, and serve warm.
